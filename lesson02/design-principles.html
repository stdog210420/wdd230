Phoebe Lin

Visual Hierarchy

Apple Inc.

https://www.apple.com/tw/

Apple's website employs the visual hierarchy principle to guide users' attention to important content. In the mobile view, it cleverly places new product releases, featured items, and the latest updates at the top of the page. It attracts users' attention through large images and clear headlines, creating a distinct visual hierarchy.

Hick's Law

National Geographic

https://www.nationalgeographic.com/

National Geographic's website utilizes the rule of thirds by placing focal points at the intersections. For instance, captivating images and headlines are positioned in the upper and left parts of the screen, creating a balanced and engaging layout.

White Space and Clean Design

Amazon

https://www.amazon.com/

Amazon's website employs the principle of proximity by placing related product elements close to each other, forming visual groups. This approach makes it easier for users to understand the relevance of different products.
